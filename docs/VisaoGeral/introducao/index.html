Introduction

If you are a developer and want to integrate your website with your Efí digital account to issue your invoices automatically, you are in the right place!

This documentation is intended for developers who want to integrate their website or application with the Efí API. API functionalities, modules, technical parameters, videos in the form of classes on integrations with our API and availability of ready-made example codes to facilitate your development activities are described.

Efí is a Payment company (IP) authorized by the Central Bank of Brazil. With our solutions, you can issue charges to your customers - boletos, carnets, payment links and subscriptions - and carry out financial transactions in your Efí Digital Account.


Collection Postman Efí Charges API#

This is the link to our Collection API Billing that contains the endpoints of all our services offered. https://documenter.getpostman.com/view/13574984/Uz5ArJQc

1. Discover our course#

Thinking about offering new means of transmitting information, Efí launched the Online Course at Efí University, which are videos in class format with the objective of explaining, in a clear and objective way, how to use our online payment solutions and integrate your system or application with the Efí API.

The course is available on the YouTube Udemy platform. The course is free and has an estimated workload of 7 hours.


2. Starting...#

Before starting, create your Efí account. An integrator can create as many applications as he wants. For each application, 2 pairs of Client_Id and Client_Secret keys are generated, one pair for use in a Production environment (?) and another for Homologation (?).

Using the Efí API, the integrator can generate transactions (payment via boleto or credit card), recurring charges, carnets and receive notifications (callbacks) whenever there are status changes in transactions.

To create your first application, log in in your Efí account, access API > Aplicações > Criar Nova Aplicação (?), enter a name for your application and click create new application (?). You will see the Client_Id and Client_Secret Production and Development keys. We recommend that you start by exploring our Playground to learn about the payment mechanism and flow in a 100% testing and hassle-free environment. In this place, all the endpoints made available by the API will be located, that is, everything that can be done via integration.

Via the Request History sub-tab (?) it is possible to view all requests made by your management system or integration with the Efí API. Learn more on the page Interpretando Erros na API.In view of the variety of integrations with Efí, we have gathered information in this table to make your choice easier.

So choose the best integration according to your needs.

3. Libraries#

Our API is RESTful and requests/responses are in JSON. Efí uses OAuth to provide authorized access to the API. All libraries available here are already prepared to perform this authentication automatically.

4. Features offered by the Efí API#

Learn how to install our API#

See how easy it is to install our SDK and integrate it into your website or system. We provide in more than 12 (twelve) languages:



Create transaction and pay with boleto or credit card#

Receive from your customers without bank bureaucracies. All slips and booklets generated by Efí are registered. Know more:



Recurrence charges (Subscriptions)#

Cover your customers on a recurring basis. As soon as he authorizes the debits, you don't have to worry about sending the charge every month and your client doesn't run the risk of forgetting to make the payment. It is possible to use both credit card and bank slip. Know more:



API Test Environment (sandbox)#

Before putting an application into production, it's very important to make sure your implementation is correct and your integrations are communicating the way they should.

To prevent integrators from having to test in production, Efí offers a validation/testing environment (sandbox), which we call "Playground". This is a place that the integrator can use to learn about the mechanism and the payment flow in a 100% test and uncomplicated environment. Know more:



MarketPlace Efí#

The marketplace concept is simple. It is a virtual environment in which several different sellers come together to offer different products and services, all in a single location, such as a shopping center. At the end of the sale in a marketplace, the automatic transfer of the value will be destined to all those involved, according to the rules previously defined by the integrator. Know more:



Partners Integrated Systems#

Official Efí partner systems whose integration with our API is native. Thus, you can use the services offered by Efí in a simple and fast way, just filling in some configurations in your system, according to the manual of the company that owns the software.


Modules and Plugins for various platforms#

Payment Link#

This feature allows you to create a link to an Efí payment screen. With this, the integrator does not need to implement its own payment screen, being able to define the payment methods it wants to allow (boleto and/or card) and, with the generated link, the payer will be redirected to the Gerencianet payment screen.
